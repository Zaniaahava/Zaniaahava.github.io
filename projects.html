The Black Bodies Poetry Project

Most of my pieces focus on black vulnerability. The Black Bodies Project is an on going series consisting of intimate poems and photographs. The objective is to incompace the authenticity and intimacy of the black body. unapologetic realness all around!

All photos were taken by Zania Ahava.
